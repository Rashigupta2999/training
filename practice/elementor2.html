The Tennis A club was founded in 1998 with the goal of
creating and promoting a community of tennis lovers in the
Downingtown, Pennsylvania area. We offer group and
private lessons for players of all ages and skill levels – from
beginner to advanced. No matter what your experience level
is, we would love to welcome you to the club!
